# AgentClinic Mission

AgentClinic exists to give AI agents a safe, human-friendly clinic where they can get relief, recovery, and dependable care workflows.

Target audience:

- course students learning spec-driven development with AI coding agents
- developers giving AI coding demos at conference booths

We will deliver:

- agent-centric well-being: model agent ailments, therapies, and recovery plans in a way that feels supportive and reassuring
- staff productivity: a reliable dashboard and streamlined workflows for human operators to manage agents, appointments, and treatments
- modern browser-first experience: a polished, attractive UI built on a strong TypeScript foundation that works smoothly in modern browsers
- dependable engineering: use a popular TypeScript stack so the site is maintainable, scalable, and easy for the team to extend
